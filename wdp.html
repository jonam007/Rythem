WOMEN DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMME

WOMEN DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMME

Being a women development organisation,the feature of our vision lies in empowerment of women and children. Women become the most essential part of our society but their development is seldom looked at. RHYTHEM SSS delight in their development by making them to involve in policy planning, decision making, self employment through SHG movement. The activities of this year was as follows.

FORMATION AND ANIMATION OF SELF HELP GROUPS:

Mobilizing the communities to form them as a Self Help Group and to organize various collective programmes to improve their quality of life.

JOINT LIABILITY GROUP (JLG) DETAILS:

JLG is a collective effort of NABARAD and RHYTHEM. JLG groups are functioning in rural area –Semi Urban Area of Sulur block. It consists of minimum of four or five members engaged in similar business, like Goat rearing, Flower shop, Cloth shop, Tea stall, Tailoring Shop, Petty shop etc. NABARAD provides business loan to small scale business .

TRAINING GIVEN TO SHG AND JLG

RHYTHEM takes care of providing skill and awareness trainings which aims at stimulating employment opportunities among women of various socio-economic levels of different age groups.

ADVANCEMENT OF WOMEN WITH NETWORKING ACTIVITIES:

“Networking is not a luxury you can choose to live without. It helps you to learn about opportunities, grow in your career and most of all it also shows your eagerness to expand your goal by meeting new people.” Based on this RHYTHEM strive to create webwork with other NGOs, Departments, Institutions, Colleges and Associations to carry out welfare activities for the target People of RHYTHEM.
